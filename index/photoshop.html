Adobe Photoshop

The world’s leading image editing and graphic design software. Photoshop is a creative powerhouse used by designers, photographers, and artists globally.

History of Photoshop

Early Beginnings

Photoshop was created in 1987 by Thomas and John Knoll. Originally a simple image editing program, it was licensed to Adobe Systems in 1988 and officially released as Photoshop 1.0 in 1990.

Growth & Evolution

Over the decades, Photoshop evolved from a photo editing tool into a complete design ecosystem. Each version introduced powerful tools like layers, masks, smart objects, and filters.

Modern Photoshop

Today, Photoshop is integrated with Adobe Creative Cloud, offering AI-powered features, cloud storage, and seamless collaboration. It remains the industry standard for visual creativity.

Key Features of Photoshop

Layers

Work non-destructively with multiple elements stacked in layers.

Selection Tools

Magic Wand, Pen Tool, and advanced selections for precise editing.

Retouching

Healing brush, clone stamp, and AI tools for photo enhancement.

Typography

Custom text effects, typography design, and font integration.

Digital Art

Brushes, painting tools, and blending for digital illustration.

3D & Effects

Create 3D text, lighting, and apply countless filters & effects.
