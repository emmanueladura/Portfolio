What is Graphic Design?

Definition

Graphic Design is the practice of creating visual content to communicate messages. Using typography, imagery, color, and layout, graphic design blends art and technology to inspire and inform.

Importance

From branding to advertising, graphic design shapes perception. It helps businesses stand out, tells stories visually, and ensures messages are clear and memorable.
